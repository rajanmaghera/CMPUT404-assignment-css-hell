About

I am a Canadian content creator making content about technology. I am the founder and CEO of Linus Media Group, a company that produces content for YouTube, Twitch, and other platforms. I also founded Floatplane, an online monetized streaming service for premium content. I am also well known for dropping anything I can get my hands on.

Professional History

Before 2008, I was an employee of NCIX, a now defunct retailer in Canada. While at NCIX, I starred in their online videos uploaded to YouTube. Since then, I have been a full-time content creator.
Year Description
2008 Started Linus Tech Tips channel.
2013 Founded Linus Media Group, a company to house all of my media ventures, and started the WAN show, a weekly podcast.
2015 Moved Linus Media Group to our new warehouse/headquarters and started TechQuickie, a sister channel to LTT focused on quick how-to's and informative content.
2018 Started the TechLinked channel, focused on up to date bite-sized tech news.
2020 Started the ShortCircuit channel, focused on short snippets of content like unboxings or first looks.
